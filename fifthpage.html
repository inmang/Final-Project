Here are some tips to achieve greater happiness

Hold onto your values

What you find value in helps guide your actions. If you stick to your values you will be more content with your decisions.

Do what you love

Everyday may not be perfect, but if you spend a little time doing things you find joy in, you will find overall greater happiness

Don't be afraid to take risks

Life is all about making decisions. If you believe you can find greater happiness doing something else, take the risk and go for it. You miss 100% of the shots you don't take.

Live in the moment

Try to avoid being fixated on past failures and instead focus on what you learned from them and it can help you in the future. Appreciate what's around you today because you never know when it may be gone.

Stop comparing yourself to others

Everyone's journey in life is different. People go through different stages at different times and may not even have the same destination as you. Too much competition can lead to jealousy so instead focus on yourself and achieving your own goals.

Stop stressing about expectations

Worrying too much about expectation can cause a lot of stress in life. Some things that you can't control may pop up. It's ok to not meet every expectation.
